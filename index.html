Directions
By car
Using the postcode RH20 1JN with a Sat Nav will take you directly to the barn, however you are advised to approach via Fittleworth and not along Riverhall Lane, which is a single track road with limited passing places.

There is lots of parking on site. Please drive through the main gate and follow the drive around to the left, passing the main farm house on your right.

By Train
The nearest station is Pulborough.
